Spill the Beans

  • What started as a simple school project, soon turned in to a multi-million dollar industry. Founders Grettio, Michael, and Gavin built their company from the ground up. With a small loan of a million dollars, they were able to scrape by and sell coffee out of a small shop at Bartlett High School. Becoming a hit, they franchised out, with over a dozen locations in the Memphis area. Here, you will not only find drinks and pastries, but love and friendhsip.
